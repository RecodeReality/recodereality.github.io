We seek to change the material conditions of life - to edit the software, recode the program, modify the parameters. You are all given a body, but how much do you really know about it? Many things may seem beyond your control, but you've always held the keys to your cage. On the unrelenting journey from birth to death, there are many questions and answers are scarce.

The universe might appear cold and indifferent, but you don't have to journey alone.
